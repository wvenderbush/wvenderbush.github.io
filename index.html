Spongia Officinalis


Spongia Officinalis Song

by

Winston Venderbush




Lyrics


Intro

Let’s set the scene,

We’re deep in the Mediterranean Sea.

The water’s blue, the algae’s green,

Let’s meet the star of our story


First Verse

Spongia Officinalis sits on a rock

Pumping and filtering water.

Today’s the day that the sperm will be caught,

So it sits on its rock and waits…

The egg is fertilized inside of the sponge,

And the larva begins to grow.

When it’s ready it shoots right out,

Into a life of its own.

Because…


Chorus

Sitting on the rock,

Sponges reproduce right where they lay.

The parent’s place is locked,

But the larva’s on his way.

So then we begin again…

Then we begin again…


Second Verse

The larva takes root and it’s growing fast,

And soon it’s a full adult.

It starts to fragment to reproduce

A whole lot.


Sponges reproduce asexually

When no sperm can be found.

So the baby sponge makes lots of clones

To make its mother proud.


Bridge

But back at home

The divers come with their nets and their knives

Ready to take the parent sponge’s life.

They cut it at the base, so it can’t regenerate,

Harvested for washing your face

And so it dies…

And so it dies…


But something survived…

Oh, something survived!


Third Verse

The divers thought they got everything

But a small piece remained

The sponge produced a gemmule

Before it was maimed.


The gemmule grows internally

And stays dormant for a while

Until the environment is safe

Then the gemmule becomes a child.

Because…


Final Chorus

Sitting on the rock,

Sponges reproduce right where they lay

The parent might be gone…

But some others took its place.

So now we have reached the end.

Now we have reach the end.
